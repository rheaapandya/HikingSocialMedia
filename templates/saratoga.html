Saratoga Gap Trail

Head out on this 4.8-mile loop trail near Los Gatos, California. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 2 h 28 min to complete. This trail is great for hiking and running, and it's unlikely you'll encounter many other people while exploring. The trail is open year-round and is beautiful to visit anytime.
